Deployment Note

Two-Phase Rollout: Email First, SMS When A2P Clears

A2P 10DLC verification for outbound SMS is not yet complete for Restaurant Lucie. The email sequence below is designed to stand on its own — fully effective without any SMS touches. When A2P clears, the planned SMS touches (shown throughout as dashed cards) activate and layer in for a lift in open and response rates.

What this means practically: Phase 1 (launch now) is the email-only sequence. Phase 2 (post-A2P) adds the two SMS touches in Segment A and the instant-text openers in Segments B & C. Nothing in the email cadence depends on SMS firing — each email does its job alone.
