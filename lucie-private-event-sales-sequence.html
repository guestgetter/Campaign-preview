Execution

Deployment Playbook

The tactical details that make the difference between a sequence that performs and one that sits in a folder.

Send Platform
If HighLevel is configured for Lucie, use it for B & C automation. Segment A should be sent manually from a personal inbox for maximum deliverability and reply rate.
Sender Name
Segment A: Personal name (Maggie)
Segments B & C: "Restaurant Lucie" or "The Lucie Team"
Optimal Send Times
Tuesday–Thursday, 10:30 AM or 6:00 PM ET. Avoid Monday morning and Friday afternoon — open rates crater.
Reply Handling
Segment A: Same-day personal response, always.
B & C: Templated reply with direct booking link within 4 hours.
Suppression Rules
Immediately suppress anyone who books, unsubscribes, or replies "not interested." Remove internal emails (@restaurantlucie.com) before launch.
Tracking
Tag each lead with sequence step in PerfectVenue / OpenTable. Track: open rate, reply rate, booking rate, revenue attributed per segment.
