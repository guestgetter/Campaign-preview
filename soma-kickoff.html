The objective
Find out exactly what it costs Soma to acquire a customer through paid digital. Build a measurable system around Free Broth Friday so every dollar in produces a trackable dollar out. In 90 days, the numbers either tell a story that makes everyone want to keep going — or they don't, and Soma walks away with more clarity than before.
The 90 days
Weeks 1–2 · Now

Wire it up

  • Meta Pixel + Conversions API on Shopify
  • Google Analytics cleanup
  • QR capture system for Free Broth Friday & demos
  • Email/SMS capture flow built
  • Baseline metrics documented
Weeks 3–8 · Run

Launch & learn

  • Free Broth Friday Meta campaign live

  • Optimize toward cost-per-lead & cost-per-purchase
Weeks 9–12 · Prove

Read the numbers

  • Full cost-per-customer report
  • Channel comparison: Meta vs. in-store vs. email
  • Winning audience & creative identified
  • Clear recommendation: scale, adjust, or stop
  • Peak season plan for September if numbers justify it
