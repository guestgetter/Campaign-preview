My Henny Penny fryer #2 is throwing error code F-12 and the oil isn't getting hot enough. What should I do?

E

Error Code F-12 on your Henny Penny OFG-341 indicates a temperature sensor failure. Here's what's happening and what to do:

Immediate Steps:

  • Power down the fryer immediately to prevent further damage
  • Do not attempt to bypass the sensor - this is a safety-critical component
  • Check that the sensor probe connection at the back of the control board is secure

Likely Cause: The RTD temperature sensor has degraded. This is common after 18-24 months of heavy use. Your unit has been in service since March 2024.

Good news: The replacement sensor (Part #HP-60828) is in stock at our Toronto warehouse and available for next-day delivery at your negotiated price of $289.00 (MSRP $345.00).
