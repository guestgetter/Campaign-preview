Event Terms

Policies & Common Questions

The following terms and policies apply to all private events at Lucie. Please review before inquiring.

A 50% deposit of the minimum spend is required to confirm and secure your booking. The remaining balance of the estimated charges is due on the day of the event.
Each space has a food & beverage minimum — the required spend before taxes and gratuity. If the total spend does not reach the minimum, the remaining balance will be applied as a room rental fee.
A 20% service gratuity is applied to all food and beverage charges, in addition to applicable taxes.
Final menu selections and adjustments must be confirmed at least 7 business days before the event. Changes after this point may result in additional charges.
Final guest counts must be confirmed prior to the event deadline. This number will be considered the guaranteed attendance for billing purposes.
Yes. Guest dietary restrictions should be submitted at least 72 hours before the event.
Yes. Our culinary team is happy to curate a customized menu for your event depending on group size and event format.
Restaurant Lucie is the exclusive provider of all food and beverages for events. Outside food, alcohol, or catering is not permitted unless expressly approved in writing by the venue.
Outside alcohol is welcome — please note there is a $75 corkage fee for every 750ml bottle of wine. Outside liquor is not permitted.
Yes, outside celebration cakes may be permitted with advance approval from the venue. A $10 per person cake plating fee will apply to cover cutting, plating, and service.
Yes, décor is welcome. However, it must be approved by the event manager in advance and must follow venue guidelines. Items such as confetti, glitter, or adhesive décor (tape, nails, tacks) are not permitted.
Open flames are not permitted unless enclosed and approved by the venue in advance.
Nothing may be attached to the walls or surfaces of the venue without prior approval from the event manager, as this may damage the space.
All rentals such as tables, linens, chairs, dishware, and AV equipment are typically coordinated through Lucie and its preferred vendor partners. Third-party rentals require written approval in advance.
Yes. Entertainment or speakers are welcome; however, all AV needs should be communicated in advance so the appropriate equipment can be arranged. This would only be permitted for a full restaurant buyout.
Vendor access and setup times will be specified in your event timeline. All vendors must coordinate with the event manager prior to arrival.
Events hosted at Lucie may not be ticketed or promoted publicly without written approval from the venue. Use of Lucie's logo, images, or branding must also be approved in advance.
Yes, photography is welcome. However, commercial filming, branding activations, or media distribution must be approved by the venue in advance.
