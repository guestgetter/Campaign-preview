01 Phase 1

The Growth Audit

Find out what's working, what's not, and where the biggest opportunity is.

Runs Every 90 Days · Days 1–14

Before we build anything, we need to know exactly where things stand. This is a full check-up on how Soma gets customers, keeps customers, and makes money — so every decision after this is based on real numbers, not guesses.

This isn't a one-time thing. We run this audit every 90 days. That way, we always know what's working, what stopped working, and where to focus next. It's the heartbeat of the partnership.

Where are customers coming from?

Walk-ins, Google, social, word of mouth, wholesale partners. We figure out which sources bring buyers — not just browsers.

What you get → You stop spending time on channels that aren't producing and double down on what is.

What happens on the website?

We track the full path: visits → browses → adds to cart → buys (or leaves). With 1,200 sessions and a 2.5% conversion rate, we find exactly where people drop off.

What you get → Small fixes in the right spots can double online revenue without spending a dollar more on ads.

Are customers coming back?

How many first-time buyers reorder? How often? What brings them back — or loses them? With $30–60 average orders, repeat buyers are where the real money comes from.

What you get → A customer who buys once is worth $50. One who comes back monthly is worth $600+. This shows us how to keep more of them.

What does the market look like?

Who else is in the broth space? What are they doing, what are they saying, and where are the gaps Soma can own?

What you get → You find the lanes nobody else is in — instead of competing where bigger brands outspend you.

What are the real economics?

Cost to get a customer. Lifetime value. Average order. Margins by channel — because a local pickup at 25%+ margin is very different from shipping glass jars to Northern Ontario.

What you get → You'll know exactly how much you can spend to get a customer — and what you make back.

What's the #1 bottleneck?

After looking at everything, we name the single biggest thing holding Soma back. Then we build the next 90 days around fixing that one thing.

What you get → You focus all your energy on the one move that has the biggest impact. One bottleneck at a time.

03 Phase 3

The 45-Day Testing Sprint

Find out what works before you spend real money.

Recurring Every 45 Days · Days 15–60

This starts while the world build is still in progress — as soon as tracking is live and the first creative is ready, we start testing. We've done this with as little as $500 in ad spend.

This is how we answer the question you raised: "What is the key benefit we will sell?" We don't guess. We test. The market tells us. And this sprint recurs — every 45 days, new tests, new intelligence.

Round 1 — What message hits?

5–10 different ad angles on Meta. Protein. Collagen. Gut health. Clean cooking. Post-surgery recovery. The market picks the winner.

What you get → You find out what makes people click. Every future ad, email, and post uses language that actually works.

Round 2 — Who responds best?

Winning messages shown to different groups. Women 35+. Post-medical. New moms. Athletes. Geo-targeted within 10–15km of Pape Ave.

What you get → You stop guessing who your best customer is. The data tells you.

Round 3 — What gets them to buy?

Winning message + best audience → test offers. Free shipping thresholds. Starter bundles. First-order incentives. 4-pack entry points.

What you get → You know what it costs to get a customer — and whether that cost pays for itself.
