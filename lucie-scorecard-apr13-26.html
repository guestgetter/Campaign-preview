How revenue is measured
Google Ads
Google + OpenTable
Google reports the count of reservations from ad clicks but not which specific ones. We estimate revenue as reservations × avg guest spend. For bookings flagged as "Reserve with Google" in OpenTable, we use the actual bill on file.
Meta — Leads
Meta + OpenTable
When someone submits a Meta lead form, we save their email/phone. We then check OpenTable history — if that same person reserved after submitting, we count it as a converted lead. Real bill where available, average spend where not.
Meta — OT Pixel
OpenTable booking page
A tracking tag fires on the OpenTable booking page when someone arrives directly from a Meta ad and completes a booking. Real bill where available, average spend where not.
