Retention

Different customers get different paths. No more blasting the same message to everyone.

— New Customer Follow-Up → drive visit 2 ASAP
— Red Carpet VIP — high spenders, high frequency
— 45-day lapse follow-up
— 90-day lapse follow-up
— Monthly newsletter
— Franchise inquiries — completely separate pipeline
— Review automation — happy → Google, unhappy → you first

Measurement

Know what every channel actually cost you — not clicks, not impressions. Real customer value, repeat behavior, and margin.

— Trace the path: ad click → first visit → repeat → lifetime value
— ROI per channel, per location
— Which promos drive real repeat customers vs. one-time discount traffic that never comes back
— Spend decisions based on profit, not volume

Key principles

Own the customer

Platforms stay for discovery. Repeat customers order direct — first-party delivery, first-party data. The economics work and the data stays in your system.

Profit over volume

Stop spending where the economics don't work. A retained customer who orders 1PD is worth more than 10 subsidized one-time discount transactions.
