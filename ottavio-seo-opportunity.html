Suggested build order

A reasonable sequence if Ottavio wants to take this on. Each phase compounds on the one before. Most of the value sits in phase 1 — winning local searches that almost no one is competing for.

Phase 1 · Weeks 1–4

Local pages and homepage

  • Rebuild homepage and About page to rank for "ottavio victoria" and "ottavio oak bay" (380 Vic/mo combined)
  • Dedicated /italian-deli-victoria landing page (1,180 Vic/mo across that family)
  • /catering landing page ("catering victoria", "charcuterie catering" — 960 Vic/mo)
  • Bakery section page targeting "bakery victoria" and "bakery near me" (10,400 Vic/mo — the largest single bucket in the data)
  • Google Business Profile: hours, attributes, weekly posts, photos for every product
Phase 2 · Weeks 5–8

30 main product pages

  • Template at /shop/[category]/[slug], live Square price feed
  • 500–800 words per product covering: what it is, where it's from, how to use it, who in Ottavio's case carries it
  • Product schema markup with price and availability
  • FAQ schema with 3–5 answered questions per product
  • 3 photos per product: counter, cut close-up, plated pairing
Phase 3 · Weeks 9–12

Long tail and automation

  • Auto-generate lighter pages for every Square item above $5
  • Editorial pass on the next 50 named products
  • Monthly "what's in the case this week" page driven by Square updates
  • Recipe pages linked back to ingredient product pages
  • Skip the keywords flagged in "search terms that point at someone else" — don't waste SEO effort there

How the numbers were sourced

Catalog
Pulled directly from Ottavio's Square Catalog API in production. 1,263 items across 69 Square categories. Of those, 657 are deli, bakery, or pantry items suitable for product pages.
Search volume
DataForSEO's Google Ads Search Volume API, live data. Two locations: Canada (location_code 2124) and Victoria, BC (location_name "Victoria,British Columbia,Canada"). 1037 keywords queried per location. Volume numbers are from Google's own Keyword Planner.
Difficulty + competitors
DataForSEO Labs keyword difficulty endpoint (0–100 score) for 66 priority keywords, plus live Google SERP scrapes for 29 of those keywords from Victoria, BC. The SERP data captures the actual top 10 organic results, presence of Google's local pack (map results) and AI Overview, plus Ottavio's current ottaviovictoria.com position where it ranks in the top 10.
Filter for shared names
Any term where Victoria volume is more than 5% of Canada volume — and which doesn't already contain a location word like "victoria" or "oak bay" — gets flagged. Population share puts Victoria at about 1% of Canada, so 5%+ usually means another local business shares the name. This caught Fol Épi (82% of CA volume is in Victoria) and Lumache restaurant (36%).
Traffic estimate
Conservative capture rates by intent strength. Branded searches: 60–80% (Ottavio should own its own name). Local searches: 15–30% (low competition). Victoria product searches: 10–25%. Canada product searches: 0.3–1.5%. These are tier ceilings assuming the pages are built properly and indexed by Google.
Refresh cadence
The script that produced this report can be re-run on demand. Running it monthly would catch seasonal patterns — charcuterie demand spikes in November/December, gelato in May/August.
