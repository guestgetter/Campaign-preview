14 · Method

Sources, parameters, and the revenue formula.

Catalog
Ottavio's Square Catalog API. 1,267 items returned by the API; 910 are live and sellable today after applying the same filters production uses (excludes 25 archived, 13 hidden, 18 off-location, and 301 with no sellable variation — sold out, variable price, etc.). Of the live items, 827 are deli, bakery, or pantry.
Search volume
DataForSEO Google Ads Search Volume API, live data. Canada (location_code 2124) and Victoria, BC (location_name "Victoria,British Columbia,Canada"). 1037 keywords per location. Volume numbers are from Google's Keyword Planner.
Difficulty + SERPs
DataForSEO Labs keyword difficulty (0–100 score) for 66 priority keywords. Live Google SERP scrapes for 29 of those from Victoria, capturing the actual top 10 organic results plus presence of local pack and AI Overview blocks.
Shared-name filter
Terms where Victoria volume exceeds 5% of Canada volume — without a location word in the query — flagged as potential local-business name collisions. Population share puts Victoria at ~1% of Canada, so 5%+ usually means another local business has the name.
Revenue model
Per-keyword formula: monthly searches × capture rate × conversion rate × average basket × 12. Capture rates by intent — branded 60–80%, local 15–30%, Victoria product 10–25%, Canada-wide product 0.3–1.5% — applied as conservative tier ceilings. The "floor" excludes lifetime value, online shop orders, catering halo, year-2+ compounding, and brand search lift.
Refresh
The analysis can be re-run on demand. Monthly refresh catches seasonality — charcuterie peaks November–December, gelato May–August.
