Suggested build order.

A reasonable sequence. Each phase compounds on the one before. Most of the value sits in phase one — winning local searches almost no one is competing for.

Phase 1 · Weeks 1–4

Local pages and homepage

  • Homepage and About page to rank for "ottavio victoria" and "ottavio oak bay" (380 Vic/mo combined)
  • Dedicated /italian-deli-victoria landing page (1,180 Vic/mo across that family)
  • /catering landing page targeting "catering victoria", "charcuterie catering" (960 Vic/mo)
  • Bakery section page for "bakery victoria" and "bakery near me" (10,400 Vic/mo — the largest single bucket)
  • Google Business Profile: hours, attributes, weekly posts, photos for every product
Phase 2 · Weeks 5–8

30 main product pages

  • Template at /shop/[category]/[slug], live Square price feed
  • 500–800 words per product: what it is, where it's from, how to use it
  • Product schema markup with price and availability
  • FAQ schema with 3–5 answered questions per product
  • 3 photos per product: counter, cut, plated pairing
Phase 3 · Weeks 9–12

Long tail and automation

  • Auto-generate lighter pages for every Square item above $5
  • Editorial pass on the next 50 named products
  • Monthly "what's in the case this week" page driven by Square updates
  • Recipe pages linked back to ingredient product pages
  • Skip keywords flagged in section 11 — no SEO effort on competitor brand names

Method.

Every number in this report is reproducible. Nothing modeled, projected, or imagined — just live data from named APIs plus transparent multiplication.

Catalog
Pulled directly from Ottavio's Square Catalog API in production. 1,263 items across 69 Square categories. Of those, 657 are deli, bakery, or pantry items suitable for product pages.
Search volume
DataForSEO's Google Ads Search Volume API, live data. Two locations: Canada (location_code 2124) and Victoria, BC (location_name "Victoria,British Columbia,Canada"). 1037 keywords queried per location. Volume numbers are from Google's own Keyword Planner.
Difficulty + competitors
DataForSEO Labs keyword difficulty endpoint (0–100 score) for 66 priority keywords, plus live Google SERP scrapes for 29 of those keywords from Victoria, BC. The SERP data captures the actual top 10 organic results, plus presence of Google's local pack (map results) and AI Overview.
Filter for shared names
Terms where Victoria volume exceeds 5% of Canada volume — without a location word in the query — are flagged as potential local-business collisions. Population share puts Victoria at about 1% of Canada, so 5%+ usually means another local business shares the name. This caught Fol Épi (82% of CA volume in Victoria) and Lumache restaurant (36%).
Revenue model
Per-keyword formula: monthly searches × capture rate × conversion rate × average basket × 12. Capture rates by intent: branded 60–80%, local 15–30%, Victoria product 10–25%, Canada-wide product 0.3–1.5%. Conservative tier ceilings throughout. The "floor" figure excludes lifetime value, online shop orders, catering halo, year-2+ compounding, and brand search lift (section 01).
Refresh cadence
The script that produced this report can be re-run on demand. Running monthly catches seasonality — charcuterie demand spikes November–December, gelato May–August.
