What's Broken

03 · What's quietly broken

Four leaks across ~380 stores.

01· Procurement chaos

380 GMs ordering 380 ways.

Email, phone, PDF. Brand standards drift by region. Cooperative pricing is right; execution leaks at the franchise.

02· Working capital

Lead times that shouldn't exist.

A skimmer takes nine days. The GM panic-orders extras. Multiplied across hundreds of locations, it's dead capital on shelves.

03· Data dark zone

QSCC can't tune what it can't see.

Order patterns by location, region, daypart — trapped in spreadsheets. The cooperative is flying blind on adoption.

04· Speed to open

Smallwares is the slowest link.

Build-to-Suit is contracting. Every day off a new opening is a measurable win. Today, smallwares is the trailing edge.
