North Star Math

$26,381 MRR → $100K MRR at $2,000/mo retainer means ~37 new clients over 9 months. That's 4–5 closes/month. At 25% close and 70% show, that's 20–24 diagnostic bookings/month — about 5–6/week. Every creative decision is measured against that number.
