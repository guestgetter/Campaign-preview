Annual savings from reducing account manager time on QSR order processing by 50%. That's 2-3 FTEs worth of manual work redirected to growth activities.
E-Commerce Revenue Lift
2x
Doubling Shopify conversion through curated catalog, proper pricing controls, and SEO-driven traffic. Edge Essentials margins are 3-5x better than the current catalog bleed.
Portal Revenue Lock-In

Protected annual revenue per chain account through portal stickiness. Every portal deployed is a moat that competitors cannot match with a phone call.
Design-Build Pipeline
+3–5

"Ask Edge" Efficiency

Combined Year-1 Impact
$500K–$1M+
Net new value created through labor savings, revenue lift, pipeline growth, and customer retention improvement. Conservative estimate.

Assumptions

  • 3-4 account managers currently spending 60%+ time on manual QSR order processing

  • Current Shopify conversion rate well below industry average of 1.5-2.5%
  • Each chain portal locks in $2M+ in annual recurring program revenue
  • Design-build projects average $500K-$1M per engagement
  • Current organic traffic is negligible relative to market search volume
