In-Studio Bridge Script

Practitioner delivers this verbally after the $79 session. Not ad copy — a conversation.

"So based on what we found today — your [specific area] is where most of your restriction is coming from. One session can start to open that up, but to actually change how it feels day to day you really need two or three more focused sessions over the next few weeks. We have a 3-visit reset that most new clients do — it's $199 total, so your session today counts as visit one. The next two are shorter, focused sessions built entirely around what we found today. Want to go ahead and lock those in before you leave?"
  • References what they personally found — not generic
  • Positions additional sessions as clinical recommendation, not a sale
  • The $79 retroactively becomes visit one — they're already in
  • "Before you leave" creates soft urgency without pressure
  • Open question at the end — not a hard close
